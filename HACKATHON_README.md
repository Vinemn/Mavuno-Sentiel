
# Mavuno Sentinel - Hackathon Pitch & Demo Guide

## 1. One-Liner Pitch for Judges
"Mavuno Sentinel is a farmer-first intelligence platform that combines offline-ready AI diagnosis with localized market insights to boost yields by 15% and income by 20% for Kenya's smallholder farmers."

## 2. 30-Second Demo Script
*   **0:00 - 0:10 (Home):** "Meet Jane, a maize farmer in Machakos. She opens Mavuno Sentinel. Right away, she sees personalized efficiency tips: 'Mulch now to save water'."
*   **0:10 - 0:20 (Diagnosis):** "She snaps a photo of a sick leaf. Our offline AI identifies Fall Armyworm instantly and recommends a local, in-stock treatment."
*   **0:20 - 0:30 (Market & Reports):** "Finally, Jane checks the new 'Market' tab. She sees maize prices are rising in her county. The app advises her to 'Hold' for better profit, while her Weekly Report forecasts a strong yield if she acts on the pest alerts today."
